People of Color could miss their chance to vote in the upcoming Presidential election.

Dozens of states have made it more difficult to register and get to polling stations.

New rules on timing and locations of registration and voting have affected low-income People of Color more than other Americans.

Vote America is connecting volunteer drivers with voters needing rides to the polls or anywhere they need to get registered.

Our community is active in Florida, North Carolina, Pennsylvania, Virginia, and Wisconsin. Let us know if you'd like to get involved!
